Hello, my name is Con and I’m a junior front-end developer based in Dublin City. My interest for design started at a very young age. This interest in design was solidified with a four-year bachelor of Science degree in Product Design. In 2016 I decided to go back to college to study Web Technologies.
Soon after I graduated, I was contracted as a Front-end Developer with Advanced Field Solutions. This was an excellent opportunity that allowed me to improve my UI /UX Design and Front-end Development skills. I worked on projects for Pepsi Co.’s and London Underground.
In October 2017 I qualified for Google’s Front-end Developer scholarship where I furthered my programming skills & knowledge of JavaScript and JQuery.
I am currently working as freelance web developer while actively looking for a permanent junior front-end role. In the mean-time I am industrious keeping up with personal projects and upskilling in the latest technologies.
